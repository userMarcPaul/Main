Delicious Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1 tbsp of sugar
  • 1 tsp of baking powder
  • Pinch of salt

Procedures

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mix the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
  3. In another bowl, beat the eggs and then add the milk.
  4. Combine the wet and dry ingredients and mix until smooth.
  5. Pour the mixture into a greased baking dish.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until golden brown.
  7. Let it cool before serving. Enjoy!
